Create Invoice On Delivery

Sometimes in business, we need to create a direct invoice based on the delivery order. This module creates invoice from delivery order when validating delivery order. If you activate the "send invoice email when delivery validates" option then it auto sends invoices by email when you validate the delivery order.

How it works? click here to watch video

Features

  • Automatically invoice creates when delivery order validates.
  • Easy to manage invoices directly from the delivery.
  • It sends auto-email of invoice to the customer on picking validation.
  • We provide the below configurations,

    A) Create Invoice On Delivery Validate: If you enable this option then it auto-creates an invoice when validating the delivery order.

    B) Send Invoice To Customer On Delivery Validate: If you enable this option then it auto-send invoice email on picking validation.
