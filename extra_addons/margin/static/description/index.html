Margin on Product, Sales, Invoices With Margin Analysis reports Odoo Apps

This odoo apps is used to apply and calculate margin on product, sale order and invoices.After installing this odoo module/app margin is automatically calculates on products, sales and invoice with margin total amount and margin percentage both. You can also see margin in Sales Analysis Report and Invoices Analysis Report pdf reports financial accounting reports in Odoo.

Features

Margin on sale/cost price

Margin and Margin Percentage will be counted on the bases of "Sale Price" and "Cost Price".

Margin calculation on invoice line

Margin will be calculated on the paticular invoice line and also on whole invoice.

Margin calculation on sale order line

Margin will be calculated on the paticular sale order line.

Ananlysis report to display margin

Margin calculation will be displayed in analysis report.
