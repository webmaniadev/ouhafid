Margin display on POS-Point of Sales Back-end With Margin Analysis Odoo apps

If you are using a POS and want to calculate Margin on each product and want to see the total Margin, then this will be the good Odoo apps for use. Using this odoo module you will able to see the margin, profit, total profit, cost price , cost price unit for product line wise as well as it will display the total Margin while placing an POS Order. You can track the Margins not only on the POS screen, but in database also.

Features

Product Margin for POS Orders

User can see details of product margin of every ordered product in back-end POS order.

Total Product Margin

User can see details total margin of ordered product in back-end POS order.

Details of Product Margin by Category

User can see details of product margin, profit, total profit, cost price , cost price unit by category.

Product Margin Report for All POS Orders

User can see product margin, profit, total profit, cost price , cost price unit report for all POS orders in back-end.
Demo Request Video Tutorial

Show POS Margin Access Rights for User

Goto Settings -> Users & Companies -> Users in that user can show or hide POS margin option.

Show POS Margin on Each Product

User can show POS margin on each product.

Show Margin on Each Product in POS

While selecting a product the margin will calculate automatically and display along with product information. It also shows the total margin based on your chosen products in the POS screen.

Point of Sale Receipt

Show Margin on POS Order

User can show margin on each product and also view the total margin in the back-end POS order.

POS Order Report Menu

Goto Point of Sale -> Reporting -> Orders in that select Average Price, Cost Price Total, Cost Price/Unit, Margin/Profit, Total Margin/Total Profit from Measures.

POS Margin Analysis

User can use pivot report filter in that details of Average Price, Cost Price Total, Cost Price/Unit, Margin/Profit, Total Margin/Total Profit for Product by category
